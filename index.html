Dr.A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam BR was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as
the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
and studied physics and aerospace engineering. Wikipedia
